Goleta, CA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Goleta (/ɡəˈliːtə/; Spanish: ; Spanish for 'Schooner') is a city in southern Santa Barbara County, California, United States. It was incorporated as a city in 2002, after a long period as the largest unincorporated populated area in the county. As of the 2000 census, the census-designated place had a total population of 55,204. A significant portion of the census territory of 2000 did not incorporate into the new city.
